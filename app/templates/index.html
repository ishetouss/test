ABOUT ENFANT MERE

WELCOME MESSAGE FROM THE CHAIRPESON Welcome to ORGANISATION ENFANTS-MERES (OEM), We are a group of people who share a common belief that if resources are channeled appropriately, would alleviate the problem of poverty over time. Although our focus is in Rwanda, our donors, partners & volunteers come from all over the world. We pride ourselves on building bridges across cultures and national boundaries. We believe that a fundamental cause of poverty and low standard of living in the world is mass illiteracy.
